Task 3 · Vocabulary

Housing Words

💵

1. Rent

The money you pay every month to live in an apartment you don't own.

The rent for this flat is $1,200 a month.

🏦

2. Mortgage

A big loan from a bank to buy a house.

It will take us 30 years to pay off the mortgage.

👨‍💼

3. Landlord

The person who owns the apartment or house that you rent.

Our landlord is very nice and fixes things quickly.

🏡

4. Suburbs

The quiet residential areas outside the center of a city.

Families often move to the suburbs for more space.

📄

5. Lease

A legal contract to rent a property for a specific time (like 1 year).

We just signed a 12-month lease for the new place.

📦

6. Tenant

A person who pays rent to live in a house or apartment.

The previous tenant left the apartment very clean.

Task 5 · Reading

Tips for Renting in a Big City

🚆 Tip 1: Location, Location, Location
An apartment might look beautiful, but if it is far from a subway station or bus stop, your daily life will be difficult. Always check the transport links.

📸 Tip 2: Document Everything
Before you move your furniture in, take photos of every room. If there is a scratch on the floor, the landlord might try to blame you as the new tenant.

💡 Tip 3: Ask About Bills
Is heating included in the rent? What about internet and water? A cheap apartment can become very expensive if you have to pay all the utilities yourself.

Task 7 · Grammar Theory

Articles: A, An, The, or Zero (-)

1️⃣ A / An (Indefinite)

Use for one non-specific thing. It's new information to the listener.

✅ "I am looking for an apartment." (Any apartment, not a specific one yet)
✅ "She bought a house."
🎯 The (Definite)

Use for a specific thing. Both the speaker and listener know which one.

✅ "The apartment I rented is very big." (The specific one we are talking about)
✅ "Can you open the window?" (The window in this room)
🚫 Zero Article (-)

Use for plural nouns or uncountable nouns when talking about them IN GENERAL.

✅ "(-) Houses in London are very expensive." (All houses generally)
✅ "I love (-) modern architecture." (Uncountable, general)
