Task 4

Grammar: Conditionals 2 & 3 🧠

Talking about "Imaginary Business Scenarios" vs. "Past Reflections".

Type Concept Structure Example
2nd Conditional Hypothetical Present
(Imagining business is different now)		 If + Past Simple, ... would + Verb "If we had a bigger budget, we would expand faster."
3rd Conditional Past Reflection
(Analyzing past outcomes)		 If + Past Perfect, ... would have + PP "If they had invested earlier, they would have made a profit."

Your Turn: Complete the Thoughts ✍️

2nd Conditional (Dreaming):

If I were the CEO of my current company, I [finish the sentence].

2nd Conditional (Hypothetical):

If we had an unlimited budget for our next project, we [finish the sentence].

3rd Conditional (Reflection):

If I hadn't gone into the finance sector, I [finish the sentence].

3rd Conditional (Past Choice):

If I had bought Bitcoin 15 years ago, I [finish the sentence].

Task 5

Game: Would You Rather? 🤔

Use 2nd Conditional to answer. "If I had to choose, I would..."

1. Skills

Would you rather be completely fluent in 5 languages OR be a master at predicting financial markets?

2. Work Style

Would you rather work 4 days a week for 12 hours a day OR 5 days a week for 6 hours a day?

3. Meetings

Would you rather lead a crucial client meeting completely unprepared OR give a rehearsed speech to 1000 people?

4. Outdoors

Would you rather spend a month camping in the deep wilderness OR a month in a luxury hotel in a chaotic city?

Task 6

Deep Dive: Travel & Culture 🌍

Let's use 3rd Conditional (Past) and general narrative tenses to discuss your travels.

🏠 Culture Shock

Think about when you first traveled or moved abroad. If you had known then what you know now, what would you have done differently?

✈️ The Best Trip

Describe your favorite travel experience. Why was it special? (The food? The people? The landscape?)

🚫 The Disaster

Have you ever had a travel disaster? (Missed flight, lost luggage). If it hadn't happened, how would the trip have been different?

🎯 The Future

If you could live in any country for 1 year (working remotely in finance), where would it be?
