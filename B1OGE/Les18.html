As a result, massive traffic congestion now cripples urban thoroughfares every single morning. Essential utility infrastructure—like power networks and transit systems—is constantly pushed to its breaking point.

Consequently, a counter-revolution is unfolding. Quiet, quaint villages are being discovered by remote workers. However, as wealthy professionals move in, rapid local gentrification is triggered.

Original residents are often pushed out as housing costs skyrocket. To preserve community equity, new sustainable urban zone templates are designed by forward-thinking planners to ensure structural balance.

05. Structural Blueprint

Passive Voice: Present & Past Simple

In city design, the action itself completely eclipses the person performing it. Let's look at this structural system setup.

The Formula Matrix

Object + TO BE (am/is/are/was/were) + Past Participle (V3)
Tense Architecture Formula Setup Clinical Example
Present Simple Passive is / are + V3 "Smart data is collected by street sensors daily."
Past Simple Passive was / were + V3 "The old transit tunnel was closed back in 2018."
Modal Integration (Revision) can / must + be + V3 "New grids can be optimized safely under our rules."

⚠️ Syntax Safety Note: Avoid structural drops. Never omit the auxiliary marker. Do not say: *“The subway line opened last year to reduce traffic”* if you mean external engineers built it. It must read: *“The line was opened.”*
