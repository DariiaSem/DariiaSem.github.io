Task 4

Storytelling: Past Simple 📖

We use the Past Simple to talk about finished actions in the past.

Sentence Type Rule Example
Positive (+) Verb + ed (or irregular verb) "I went to the park yesterday."
Negative (-) did not (didn't) + base verb "I didn't go to the park."
Question (?) Did + subject + base verb? "Did you go to the park?"

Your Turn: Tell me a story! ✍️

Yesterday:

Tell me 3 things you did yesterday. (e.g., I woke up at 7. I drank coffee. I worked.)

Last Weekend:

Where did you go last weekend? Who did you meet?

Task 5

Fun Choices 🤔

Which one do you prefer? Tell me your opinion.

1. Food

Would you rather eat pizza every day OR never eat chocolate again?

2. Travel

Would you rather travel to the past OR travel to the future?

3. Money vs Time

Would you rather have a lot of money but no free time OR a lot of free time but little money?

4. Superpowers

Would you rather fly like a bird OR breathe underwater like a fish?

7. Daily Routine (Phrasal Verbs)
